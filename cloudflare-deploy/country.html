FAQ

Good to know

How fast is the appointment secured?+
Most centres open slots within days. Once you're on our watch list, we book the moment one appears, usually within 48 hours.
What if my visa is refused?+
Our fee covers the appointment and document work, the visa decision sits with the embassy.
When do I pay?+
Never before your appointment is confirmed. Payment is only collected once your slot is secured.
I'm self-employed or a student, can you still help?+
Yes. We'll guide you through the right proof for your situation, an accountant's letter and self-assessment for self-employed applicants, or an enrolment letter for students.
Can you help with a visa for my child?+
Yes. Minors need a birth certificate, parental consent, and copies of both parents' passports, we'll tell you exactly what applies to your family.
