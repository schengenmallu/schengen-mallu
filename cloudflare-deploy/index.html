Still wondering?

You've probably got questions.

We'd rather answer them now than leave you guessing. Here's everything people ask us before they start.

How fast can you get me an appointment?+
Timelines vary by destination and availability. Fast Track cases activate same day. We're upfront about what's achievable.
What does it cost?+
Quoted on WhatsApp based on tier, destination, and urgency. You pay only after appointment confirmation.
What if you can't find me a slot?+
Full refund. No slot, no fee, every tier.
How does the Guaranteed refund work?+
If the embassy refuses your visa, we refund in full. Subject to eligibility review. If we don't think it'll be approved, we tell you upfront.
Do I check my application before it's used?+
Always. Nothing is finalised until you confirm every field is correct.
What happens to my documents after?+
Securely deleted 6 months after you share them with us, per our GDPR Policy. You're notified when it happens.
